VUE AUTOMOTIVE - AURA


TWO PERSONALITIES, ZERO COMPROMISE.


Whether you go for the Sport or Premium trim level, the Vue Aura is a no-compromise vehicle that caters to your desires in a sleek, luxurious, and compact package.

SMALLER SIZE = BIGGER SPACE


Our liftback design and clever packaging ensure that the Vue Aura's capabilities transcend its size class. With a boot capacity of 394L, the Vue Aura outclasses competitors that are a notch bigger.
